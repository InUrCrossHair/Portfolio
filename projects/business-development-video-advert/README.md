## Overview

Created a short-form Facebook video advertisement for the Business Development Program, designed to be minimal, informative, and attention-grabbing for social media scrollers.

## Project goal

Quickly communicate the program’s value, hold attention long enough to deliver the core message, and end with clear contact information.

## My role

- Created the video advert solo.
- Built the video using Canva clips and overlays.
- Used royalty-free clips and subtle background music.
- Generated the AI voiceover in Clipchamp.
- Timed the video clips around the voiceover audio.
- Timed captions to match the voiceover and pacing.
- Used approved/revised wording from the program director.

## Process

- Started with a minimal, scroller-friendly concept.
- Selected royalty-free visuals that fit the business development message.
- Created voiceover first, then structured timing, clips, captions, and pacing around the audio.
- Ended with contact information and a direct program call to action.
