Social Media Analytics / Excel Dashboard

2025 OMJ Facebook Analytics Dashboard

Built a full-year reporting workbook for the Pike County OMJ Facebook page, organizing Facebook-provided metrics, graphs, audience data, and top-performing content into a more navigable Excel dashboard.

Project Goal

Make a full calendar year of Facebook page analytics easier to review, navigate, and reference by organizing key numbers, embedded graphs, audience information, and top content breakdowns into a structured Excel report.

Tools: Microsoft Excel, Facebook page analytics exports, dashboard layout, charts, workbook navigation

Download full XLSX report Back to Projects

My Role

  • Built the Excel dashboard/report structure for the full 2025 calendar year.
  • Organized Facebook page metrics into clear sections for year totals, top content, and audience breakdowns.
  • Used Facebook-provided numbers and graphs to make performance trends easier to navigate and compare.
  • Created a workbook layout that supports quick movement between report pages instead of leaving the data as disconnected screenshots or exports.
  • Highlighted major metrics such as views, content interactions, follower count, watch time, and top-performing posts.

Selected 2025 Metrics

  • 5,027,261 total views
  • 21,764 content interactions
  • 11,214 current followers
  • 7 days and 17 hours of total watch time
  • Top content breakdowns by views, reach, and interactions
