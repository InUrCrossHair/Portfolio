## Overview

Designed a large-format welcome banner for Business Summit 2026, incorporating logos from the multiple groups and partners involved in the event.

## Project goal

Create a clean, clear, readable welcome banner for display on the wall during the Business Summit.

## My role

- Designed the welcome banner in Canva.
- Included logos for all different groups involved in the event.
- Focused on wall-display readability and clean output.
- Used HP Print to print the banner on the organization’s banner printer.

## Process

- Balanced event title/theme with many partner logos.
- Prioritized readability at distance for a physical event environment.
- Prepared and produced the final banner through in-house print equipment.
